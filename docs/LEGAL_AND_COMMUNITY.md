## Legal Considerations

### Why Legal Structure Matters

DAOs operate in a legal grey area. Without proper structure:
- ‚ùå Members may have unlimited personal liability
- ‚ùå DAO may not be able to sign contracts
- ‚ùå Tax treatment is unclear
- ‚ùå Regulatory compliance is difficult
- ‚ùå Banking and fiat operations are impossible

### Legal Wrapper Options

#### 1. Wyoming DAO LLC (USA)

**Pros:**
- First US state to recognize DAOs
- Limited liability for members
- Can own property and sign contracts
- Clear tax treatment
- Relatively affordable

**Cons:**
- Only recognized in Wyoming
- Annual fees required
- Must have registered agent
- US tax obligations

**Cost:** $100-500 setup + $60/year  
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

**How to Set Up:**
1. Visit https://sos.wyo.gov/
2. File Articles of Organization
3. Include DAO-specific provisions
4. Appoint registered agent
5. File annual reports

#### 2. Swiss Association (Switzerland)

**Pros:**
- Crypto-friendly jurisdiction
- International recognition
- No minimum capital required
- Flexible governance
- Privacy protections

**Cons:**
- More expensive
- Requires Swiss presence
- Complex setup
- Annual audits may be required

**Cost:** $5,000-15,000 setup + ongoing costs  
**Timeline:** 2-3 months

**How to Set Up:**
1. Draft association statutes
2. Hold founding meeting
3. Register with canton
4. Open Swiss bank account
5. Register for taxes

#### 3. Marshall Islands DAO LLC

**Pros:**
- Designed for DAOs
- International recognition
- No local presence required
- Crypto-friendly
- Low ongoing costs

**Cons:**
- Newer jurisdiction
- Less tested in courts
- May face banking challenges
- Perception issues

**Cost:** $2,000-5,000 setup + $500/year  
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

#### 4. Cayman Foundation

**Pros:**
- Well-established
- Tax neutral
- International recognition
- Strong legal framework
- Used by major crypto projects

**Cons:**
- Expensive
- Complex setup
- Requires local directors
- Ongoing compliance costs

**Cost:** $15,000-30,000 setup + $10,000+/year  
**Timeline:** 2-3 months

### Recommendation

**For Small DAOs (<$100k treasury):**
- Start with Wyoming DAO LLC
- Simple, affordable, recognized

**For Medium DAOs ($100k-$1M treasury):**
- Consider Marshall Islands or Swiss Association
- Balance cost and recognition

**For Large DAOs (>$1M treasury):**
- Cayman Foundation or Swiss Association
- Maximum legal protection and recognition

- You may lose all funds

Nothing on this platform constitutes investment advice.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
The DAO and its contributors are not liable for any losses.

## 6. Governing Law

```markdown
# Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect
- Wallet addresses (public blockchain data)
- Transaction history (public blockchain data)
- No personal information collected

## How We Use Information
- To provide DAO services
- To display governance information
- To improve the platform

## Data Storage
- All governance data is on public blockchain
- No private data is stored

## Your Rights
- You control your wallet
- You can disconnect at any time
- Blockchain data is permanent
```
